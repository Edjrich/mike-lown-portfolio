My Personal aesthetic may be best described as 'Early Man', but I assure you my Design approach is focused intently on the future...

I'm a UX Designer currently based in Toronto, ON.

I'm an advocate for making inclusive tech in a world of 'disruptive' tech. My holistic approach to design problems cultivates business value through intuitive & impactful user experiences. I’m a self-driven storyteller, and have followed a love of film & music throughout my career. Pairing my enthusiasm for tech start-up culture & my passion for creative problem-solving led me to the study of UX Design. Working on two sides of the Atlantic has fed my curiosity & helped me remain adaptable to ever-evolving industries.I’ve always believed in leaving any place in a better state than I found it, letting principles guide decision-making & seeking insight from diverse perspectives. Informed by a background in customer care, empathy is at the heart of my design approach, ensuring accessibility across established and emerging technologies.
